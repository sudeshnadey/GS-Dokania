Import Export Code Registration procedure
Step 1: Fill Application Form

To get code an application needs to be done with online application at DGFT portal. In application it will ask few information related to your business and its authorized representative or applicant.

Step 2: Preparation of required documents

Details of the business and its owners, directors or partners, bank account details. Along with basic information of director or applicants and business, we require adhaar OTP or DSC of applicant.

Step 3: IEC fee payment

Once the form is finished, it needs to be filed with DGFT and an application fee needs to be paid to the government. IEC department may provide back your application for improvement/change. You need to reply to the DGFT at earliest.

Step 4: IE Code Issuance

Once the application is completely verified by the department, the Directorate General of Foreign Trade will provide an IE Code for your organization. This IE code issues in soft copy. You get the softcopy by email and can start the import-export transaction with this.
